Lemon bars


Description

These are classic lemon bars featuring a soft butter shortbread crust and a tangy sweet lemon curd filling thatâ€™s baked to the perfect consistency. The lemon layer is thick and substantial, not thin or flimsy like most other lemon bar recipes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup + 2 cups sugar
  • 2 cups + 6 tbps Flour
  • 2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1/3 tsp Salt
  • 6 Eggs
  • 1 cup Lemon Juice

Steps

  1. Prepare crust
  2. Pre-bake crust
  3. Prepare filling
  4. Bake
  5. Cool


Eat and Enjoy!
