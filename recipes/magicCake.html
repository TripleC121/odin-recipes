Magic Cake


Description

This aptly named magic cake transforms one batter into a 3-in-1 layered dessert with a thick and fudgy bottom layer, a creamy custard center, and a fluffy layer of sponge cake on top. The magic all happens in the oven—no special ingredients or equipment required. I love the range of textures in this dessert without the work of making a crust, a custard, and a cake separately. The recipe calls for a short list of pantry staples, plus it’s easy to customize with different flavors.


Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups + 6 tbps Flour
  • 2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 4 Eggs, seperated
  • 3/4 cup All purpose Flour/li>

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven 325
  2. Heat milk and butter
  3. Beat egg whites
  4. Start the batter
  5. Bake/li>


Eat and Enjoy!
